Round of 16

As we're wrapping up the group stage of the UEFA Champions League, we're looking forward to the Round of 16. The draw for the fixtures will be made Monday, December 18, at 11am GMT/ 6am ET. For the draw, the group winners will be facing the runners-up. No side can gace another team from the same national association and teams cannot face an opponent they previously faced during the group stage.
